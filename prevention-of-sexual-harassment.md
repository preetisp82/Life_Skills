# Prevention of Sexual Harassment

## 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

* Unwelcome physical contact or advances.
* Sexual comments, jokes, or inappropriate remarks.
* Sending or showing offensive messages, images, or videos.
* Repeated unwanted requests for dates or personal meetings.
* Staring, gestures, or comments that make someone uncomfortable.
* Any unwelcome verbal, non-verbal, or physical behaviour of a sexual nature.

## 2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

* Stay calm and ensure the person's safety.
* Clearly tell the person to stop if it is safe to do so.
* Record the details of the incident.
* Report the incident to the manager, HR, or the Internal Committee (IC).
* Support the affected person and encourage them to report the incident.
* Cooperate with the investigation and maintain confidentiality.
