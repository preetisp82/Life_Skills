# Listening and Assertive Communication

## 1. What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening?

* Pay full attention to the speaker.
* Avoid interrupting.
* Observe body language.
* Ask questions for clarification.
* Summarize what was said.
* Give feedback to show understanding.

## 2. According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening?

* Listen carefully to the speaker.
* Understand the emotions behind the message.
* Repeat the message in your own words.
* Verify whether your understanding is correct.
* Avoid judging or interrupting.
* Ensure both people are on the same page.

## 3. What are the obstacles in your listening process?

* Getting distracted by phone or surroundings.
* Thinking about my response while others are speaking.
* Losing focus during long discussions.
* Missing small details.

## 4. What can you do to improve your listening?

* Give full attention to the speaker.
* Take notes during discussions.
* Avoid distractions.
* Ask questions when something is unclear.
* Confirm my understanding before responding.

## 5. When do you switch to Passive communication style in your day-to-day life?

I become passive when I hesitate to express my opinions or avoid conflicts with others.

## 6. When do you switch into Aggressive communication styles in your day-to-day life?

I may become aggressive when I am stressed, frustrated, or when work does not go as planned.

## 7. When do you switch into Passive Aggressive communication styles in your day-to-day life?

I sometimes become passive aggressive when I feel disappointed but avoid expressing my feelings directly.

## 8. How can you make your communication assertive?

* Express my thoughts clearly and respectfully.
* Listen to others before responding.
* Stay calm during disagreements.
* Use simple and direct language.
* Ask questions when I need clarification.
* Respect both my needs and others' opinions.
* Focus on solving problems instead of blaming people.
