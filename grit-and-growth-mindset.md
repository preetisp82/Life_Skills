# Grit and Growth Mindset

## 1. Paraphrase (summarize) the Grit video in a few (1 or 2) lines.

Grit is the ability to stay committed to a goal and keep working hard even when challenges and failures occur. Success comes from consistent effort and perseverance over time.

## 2. Paraphrase (summarize) the Growth Mindset video in a few (1 or 2) lines.

A growth mindset is the belief that abilities and skills can improve through learning, practice, and effort. Mistakes and challenges are opportunities to grow.

## 3. What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?

Internal Locus of Control means believing that your actions and decisions have the biggest impact on your success. The key point is to focus on what you can control instead of blaming external situations.

## 4. What are the key points mentioned by the speaker to build a growth mindset?

* Believe that abilities can improve.
* Accept challenges.
* Learn from mistakes.
* Take feedback positively.
* Keep practicing consistently.
* Replace negative thoughts with positive ones.
* Focus on progress instead of perfection.

## 5. What are your ideas to take action and build Growth Mindset?

* Practice coding every day.
* Learn from mistakes instead of getting discouraged.
* Ask for feedback and improve.
* Stay consistent with learning.
* Break large goals into smaller tasks.
* Keep trying until I understand the concept.
* Focus on continuous improvement.
