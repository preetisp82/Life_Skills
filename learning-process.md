# Learning Process

## 1. What is the Feynman Technique?

The Feynman Technique is a learning method that helps us understand concepts by explaining them in simple words.

## 2. In this video, what was the most interesting story or idea for you?

The most interesting idea to me was that both focused thinking and relaxed thinking are important for learning and problem-solving.

## 3. What are active and diffused modes of thinking?

* Active mode is focused thinking used to solve problems directly.
* Diffused mode is relaxed thinking that helps make connections and generate ideas.

## 4. According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic?

* Break down the skill into smaller parts.
* Learn enough to correct mistakes.
* Remove distractions.
* Practice for at least 20 hours.

## 5. What are some of the actions you can take going forward to improve your learning process?

* Focus completely while studying.
* Keep the phone on silent and avoid distractions.
* Understand concepts in my own words.
* Practice concepts by writing code.
* Complete tasks within deadlines.
* Communicate clearly when facing difficulties.
* Revise concepts regularly and teach others.
